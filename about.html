Francis Elizabeth Strathern

When I was in medical school, I dreamed of being a cosmetologist. But in Tselinograd then it was impossible. Everything was distributed by the state, the rate of the cosmetologist was one, and she was employed by one person for life. So I worked as a pulmonologist for six years, and then perestroika happened, the world changed, and it became possible to choose the specialization you want.

I started studying cosmetology from the very beginning, first in Kazakhstan, then in Russia and France. And so the company was born out of my girlish dream. In 1996 I opened the first beauty salon in the city of Akmola in a small two-room apartment in Khrushchev.

Our history

The first Solaris salon was opened in Tver at 60 Mozhaisky Street (building 1.2) in the Mozhaisky Shopping Center in 2002. From the very beginning, the business was focused on high standards, and therefore the first salon was built in a complex with a luxurious sauna.

There were legends about this sauna: it had an incredible pool at that time, which was decorated with a decorative bridge that emphasized the size of the pool.

Sauna and bathing lovers, despite the queues and appointments, were not the clients whose needs the business was focused on: the organizers of the salon had ambitious goals.

In a short time, the Solaris salon on Mozhaiskogo became so popular that the lack of space and the shortage of craftsmen became apparent.

In order to make the services provided by the Solaris salon available to residents of the Zavolzhsky district, in 2006 a salon was opened on Pasha Savelyeva Street, in the building of the newly built Duna Shopping Center
